Mechanic

How buyback-and-burn works

Protocol fees from box mint/redeem accumulate in the FeeCollector. A keeper can execute the buyback once a Sushi V2 BPUMP/WSOL pool exists, and purchased BPUMP is sent to the burn destination. Until that pool is seeded, fees accumulate in the collector. This page only reports live on-chain figures after contracts are configured.

Fee revenue is intended to fund an independent audit before any cap increases, ahead of BPUMP buyback-and-burn.
